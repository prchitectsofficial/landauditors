WHO WE ARE

What We Do

Land Auditors is a specialized legal auditing service designed specifically for investors and buyers looking to purchase land, plots, or constructed homes in Dehradun and surrounding areas. We understand that investing in property is one of the most significant financial decisions you'll make, and we're here to ensure you make that decision with complete confidence and legal clarity.

Our comprehensive audit process involves thorough verification of property titles, identification of any existing disputes, review of all legal documents, and deep analysis of property history. We work with empanelled lawyers who bring over 30 years of combined experience in land law, specifically focusing on properties in Dehradun and surrounding areas.

Unlike RERA-certified projects where regulatory oversight exists, independent land, plots, and constructed homes require meticulous legal scrutiny. We provide that independent, honest assessment that brokers often cannot offer, giving you the peace of mind you deserve.

OUR STORY

Our Motivation: Born From Experience

The inspiration behind Land Auditors comes directly from our founder, Prashant Sharma's personal journey. Having been scammed multiple times due to overlooking thorough legal audits, he realized how crucial it is to have a trusted third-party review when investing in land, plots or constructed homes or flats built on independent land (unlike Rera certified projects).

He saw firsthand how relying solely on broker pitches could lead to trouble, and how difficult it was to find truly independent, honest legal advice. Motivated by these experiences, he founded this service to help others avoid the same pitfalls.

Our mission is to offer investors a clean, trusted auditing service that makes the process simple and transparent, so they can protect their hard-earned money. It's all about turning a tough personal lesson into a way to help others invest safely and confidently.
